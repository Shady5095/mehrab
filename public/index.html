Welcome to Mehrab

Mehrab is a Quran memorization platform that connects teachers and students seamlessly through secure online sessions powered by Google Meet.

Teachers can create study sessions directly in the app, and students can join instantly — making Quran memorization interactive, organized, and accessible.

Our app only requests necessary permissions to schedule meetings and manage sessions securely. We value your privacy and handle all user data responsibly.
