Privacy Policy

Mehrab is a Quran memorization platform that connects students with teachers for online learning sessions. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

Information We Collect

  • Basic profile data from Google Sign-In (name, email, and profile picture).
  • Meeting data created using the Google Calendar API (session title, time, and Google Meet link).

How We Use Your Information

The collected data is used only to schedule and manage Quran learning sessions between students and teachers. We do not access or modify any other calendar data without your explicit consent. All data is securely stored in Firebase Firestore and is never shared with third parties.

Data Retention and Deletion

We retain Google user data only for as long as it is necessary to provide and improve the Mehrab service. User data (including Google profile and calendar information) is automatically deleted when a user revokes access to the app or deletes their account. Users may also request deletion of their data at any time by contacting us at shady.hesham10@gmail.com. Once deleted, all associated information is permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

Data Security

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. However, no electronic transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page, and changes will take effect immediately upon posting.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us at: shady.hesham10@gmail.com
